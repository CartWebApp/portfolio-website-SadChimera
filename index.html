Biography

Biography: “Hello, I’m Jennifer Lee, and I came to CART in 2024 for WEB AM before ending in 2025 to pursue Law & Order and Policy AM in 2025-2026. I attend Clovis East High School, and with dreamy eyes, I came here to code with the idea that I was going to be a “pro hacker.” But I learned more than simple movie stereotypes, I learned more than just typing 1s and 0s. I learned that web development is pretty arduous and frustrating at times, but an important skill too. I feel that if I never came into this classroom, I probably would have never become comfortable with wanting to make my own websites and personal projects by myself. I feel as if I would have never got to meet cool people here who are good at what they do if I didn’t go. I feel glad to be here because of new opportunities granted to me and glad of the people that changed my life. There were many hiccups and mistakes I’ve done that I will remedy in the future as I grow more confident, even if it’s slow. I feel proud for wanting to learn something that I know I want to struggle with, but learn continuously, until I am a “professional.” I want to continue to grow alongside the developing world.
